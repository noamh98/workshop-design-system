Strategy Workshop Design System

The senior consultant's notebook, in code

A reusable HTML/CSS/vanilla-JS component library for executive strategy decks — AI, SAP, Power BI, government, insurance, transportation, banking, healthcare, cyber, and executive reporting. Paper textures, a hand-sketched annotation layer, and a fixed black/blue/red/green/orange/purple ink convention sit on top of a clean, professional typographic base. No frameworks, no icon libraries — everything below is this system's own tokens, CSS, and SVG.
