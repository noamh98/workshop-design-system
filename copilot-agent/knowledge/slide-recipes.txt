=== MULTI-SLIDE DECKS ===
Duplicate the whole block per slide, inside the same wrapper div:
<section class="slide style-sketchbook" lang="he" dir="rtl" aria-label="...">
  <div class="sketch-dash"> ... </div>
</section>
Print gives one slide per page automatically.

=== ICON RULES ===
Use only icons whose <symbol> is inlined in the page. The starter ships:
icon-doc, icon-user, icon-bar-chart, icon-chip, icon-warning-fill.
More available in the repo at project/icons/sprite.svg (fetch and inline the
<symbol> you need): shield-check, search, car-fill, folder-fill, clock-fill,
and ~35 more. Never hotlink icons.
